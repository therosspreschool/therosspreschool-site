Frequently Asked Questions

What ages do you serve?
We serve children ages 2–5. Our Preschool program is for ages 2–4, and our Pre-Kindergarten program is designed for children ages 4–5.
What are your hours?
Our school day runs 8:30 am – 1:00 pm. We also offer aftercare and enrichment classes that run until 3:00 pm. We do not provide care after 3:00 pm.
What is your curriculum philosophy?
We are play-based, child-directed, and nature-oriented. We believe children learn best when they are engaged, curious, and emotionally connected. Our curriculum is built around 21st Century Skills while nurturing the whole child - intellectually, emotionally, and socially.
Is The Ross Preschool a non-profit?
Yes. We are a 501(c)3 non-profit organization (EIN: 26-4835669), founded with the mission of providing exceptional early childhood education to the children and families of our community.
How do I schedule a tour?
Use our enrollment portal to book a tour, learn about tuition, and apply - all in one place. We'd love to show you around.
What makes TRP different?
TRP is more than a preschool - it's a genuine partnership with families to raise capable, compassionate humans. Our teachers are exceptional, our community is warm and close-knit, and our curriculum is designed with intention to prepare children not just for kindergarten, but for life.
