Legal & Compliance

Privacy Policy

How The Ross Preschool collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Effective Date: April 7, 2026  |  The Ross Preschool · Lagunitas Road #114, Ross, CA 94957

Overview

The Ross Preschool ("TRP") is committed to protecting the privacy of the families we serve. This Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard personal information provided through our website, enrollment process, parent portal, and related communications.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Parent and guardian names, addresses, phone numbers, and email addresses
  • Child's name, date of birth, and relevant developmental or health information
  • Emergency contact information
  • Payment and billing information (processed through secure third-party processors)

Information Collected Automatically

  • Browser type, IP address, and pages visited (via standard web analytics)
  • Cookies and similar technologies used to improve site performance

How We Use Your Information

  • To manage enrollment, billing, and school operations
  • To communicate with you about school schedules, events, and updates
  • To send SMS notifications when you have opted in
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations

SMS Communications

The Ross Preschool sends SMS messages only to parents and guardians who have explicitly opted in. These messages are strictly operational - never marketing or promotional.

Opt-in is collected during the enrollment process via a required consent checkbox. Consent is never pre-checked and is never required as a condition of enrollment. There are no opt-in keywords - consent is enrollment-based only.

Message frequency is typically 0-4 messages per month. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out at any time. For full program details, see our SMS Alerts page.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties. Phone numbers collected for SMS are never sold, rented, or shared with any third party.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Children's Privacy

Our website is directed at parents and guardians, not children. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13.

Your Rights & Choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information by contacting us below. California residents may have additional rights under the CCPA.
