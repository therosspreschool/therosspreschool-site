SMS Notification Terms

Program Description

The Ross Preschool operates an SMS notification program for enrolled families who have opted in. Messages are strictly operational - no marketing.

Opt-In

By providing your phone number and checking the consent box during enrollment, you agree to receive SMS messages. Consent is never required as a condition of enrollment. The consent checkbox is never pre-selected. Opt-in is enrollment-based - there are no opt-in keywords.

Upon being added to the program, you will receive a confirmation message: "You are subscribed to receive important updates from The Ross Preschool (e.g., closures, alerts). Msg frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out or HELP for help."

Message Frequency & Rates

Typically 0-4 messages per month. Message and data rates may apply.

Opt-Out

Reply STOP to any message at any time.
