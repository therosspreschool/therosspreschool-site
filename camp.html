The TRP Difference

More Than Just Summer Childcare

Camp at The Ross Preschool isn't just a way to fill the summer - it's an experience kids genuinely look forward to and talk about for months after. We bring the same intentionality, warmth, and child-centered approach that defines our school year into every single camp week.

Children spend the majority of their time outside in our incredible outdoor classroom - exploring, creating, getting wonderfully messy, and building the kind of friendships that only happen when kids have real space and real time to just be kids.

Our teachers know children. They know how to spark curiosity, manage the beautiful chaos of excited preschoolers, and make every child feel seen and celebrated. That's what sets TRP camp apart.

What Kids Do All Day

Packed with Fun From
Drop-off to Pick-up

Every camp day is a mix of structured activities and free exploration - so kids stay engaged, energized, and excited to come back tomorrow.

🎨

Arts & Crafts

Painting, collage, sculpture, and process-based projects that let kids create freely and take home something they're proud of.

💧

Water Play

Splash tables, water slides, sprinklers, and all the glorious messiness that makes summer childhood unforgettable.

🔬

STEM Projects

Hands-on experiments, building challenges, and sensory exploration that make science feel like pure magic.

🌿

Nature & Garden

Tending the garden, exploring with magnifying glasses, and connecting with the natural world right in our outdoor classroom.

🎵

Music & Movement

Songs, dancing, instruments, and silly games that get every kid on their feet and grinning from ear to ear.

🏴‍☠️

Outdoor Adventure

The pirate ship, gravel pit, mud kitchen, and open-ended play spaces that spark big imagination and even bigger energy.

📚

Story Time

Engaging read-alouds, puppet shows, and dramatic play that build a love of stories and spark creative thinking.

🤝

Friendship & Play

Unstructured time to build real friendships - because some of the best learning happens when kids just play together.

Our Team

Teachers, Counselors & CITs - a Crew That Loves Kids

Camp is led by TRP's own fabulous teaching team, supported by camp counselors and counselors-in-training (CITs). It's a multigenerational crew that genuinely loves kids and knows exactly how to make a summer day feel like the best one yet.

Our ratios stay low by design. We want every child to feel known, cared for, and genuinely celebrated - not just supervised. The CIT program also gives older kids a chance to give back and grow as young leaders.

Whether your child is a TRP alumnus or joining us for the very first time, they'll feel at home within about ten minutes. That's just how camp works here.
