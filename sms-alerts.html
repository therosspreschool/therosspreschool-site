Communication & Transparency

SMS Alerts & Notifications

How The Ross Preschool communicates time-sensitive information to enrolled families.

About This Program

The Ross Preschool operates an SMS notification program exclusively for parents and guardians of currently enrolled students. Messages are strictly operational - never marketing or promotional.

What Types of Messages We Send

  • School closures or delays due to weather or power outages
  • Early dismissal notifications
  • Emergency and safety alerts (e.g., shelter-in-place)
  • Schedule changes, parking updates, or calendar reminders

Message Frequency

Typically 0 to 4 messages per month. No marketing messages ever.

How Parents Opt In to SMS Notifications

During enrollment, parents and guardians may optionally provide a mobile phone number and check an SMS consent checkbox to receive operational text alerts. This is never required to complete enrollment. The opt-in section of the enrollment form is shown below exactly as it appears to parents.

SMS Opt-In - Enrollment Form Section
