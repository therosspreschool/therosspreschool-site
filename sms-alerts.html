Communication & Transparency

SMS Alerts & Notifications

How The Ross Preschool communicates time-sensitive information to enrolled families.

About This Program

The Ross Preschool operates an SMS notification program exclusively for parents and guardians of currently enrolled students. Messages are strictly operational - never marketing or promotional.

What Types of Messages We Send

  • School closures or delays due to weather or power outages
  • Early dismissal notifications
  • Emergency and safety alerts (e.g., shelter-in-place)
  • Schedule changes, parking updates, or calendar reminders

Message Frequency

Typically 0 to 4 messages per month. No marketing messages ever.

How to Opt In

Consent is collected during enrollment via a required checkbox, or through the secure parent portal. Consent language:

"By providing your phone number, you agree to receive occasional SMS messages from The Ross Preschool related to school operations, including closures, safety alerts, and schedule changes. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for help."

How to Get Help

Reply HELP or INFO, or contact us directly. Message and data rates may apply.

Privacy & Data Use

Phone numbers are never sold, rented, or shared. See our Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions.
